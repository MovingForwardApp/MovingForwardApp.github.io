Account Limits

Free Account:

Max 10 monitors, 5 minute minimum check interval and soft limit of 300 email notifications for month (soft limit because we currently don't know the fair limit so we will learn the usage for to finally establish a definitive limits).

Email sending limits:

Right now we don't know a fair limits for email sending so all limits are soft, we don't want users to be not notified because some email limit is reached. Accounts with abusive usage will be notified via email with the intention of to find a solution or finally suspend email sending for that account.

Bounces and Complains:

In order to maintain the email notification reputation healthy, we have a strong policy for bounces and complains that consists in:

  • Each profile has a registry of incidents (bounces and/or complaints).
  • Email contact creation requires email validation.
  • Any contact bounce or complain will register a profile incident.
  • A contact has a maximum limit of 3 bounces before is permanetly blocked.
  • Blocked contacts can be deleted. Creating it again will require again the email validation and if that validation results in an other bounce an additional incident registry will be added to the profile.
  • If contact receives a complaint, it will be directly blocked.
  • Profiles with more than 5 incidents in the last 7 days will be eligible to immedite email sending suspension.

As a rule, things like “pocket-money side-projects” are considered personal, especially if they’re open source. Just get in touch if you’re unsure and I’ll tell you if it’s considered personal.
